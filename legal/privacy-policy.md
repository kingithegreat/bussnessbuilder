## 1. Scope and Roles

1.1 This Policy applies to personal information we process when you visit our marketing site, create an account, and use the Service.

1.2 **Two different roles.**
- **For your account and your use of the Service**, we act as a **data controller** (or "business" under U.S. law) for the personal information we collect about you.
- **For the enquiry/CRM data that your own website visitors and customers submit through a site you build with the Service**, **you are the data controller** of that data and **we act as your data processor (service provider)**, processing it on your behalf and under your instructions. You are responsible for having a lawful basis, providing your own privacy notice, and honoring the rights of your site's visitors. See Section 7.

## 2. Information We Collect

2.1 **Account information.** Name, email address, password (stored in hashed form by our authentication provider), and authentication identifiers when you sign up via email/password or Google sign-in.

2.2 **Business and site data.** Information you provide through the setup wizard and site builder, such as business name, description, branding, images, services, pricing, content pages, and configuration. Uploaded images are stored in cloud storage.

2.3 **Enquiry / CRM data.** Information submitted by your website visitors through enquiry forms on sites you build (e.g., name, email, phone, message), which we store on your behalf and make available to you in your inbox/CRM. See Section 7.

2.4 **Payment data.** When you subscribe to a paid plan, payment is processed by **Stripe**. Stripe collects and processes your payment card and billing details. We do **not** store full card numbers; we receive limited information such as subscription status, plan, and billing metadata.

2.5 **AI tool inputs and outputs.** Prompts and content you submit to AI features, and the generated outputs, which are processed via the AI provider (Google Gemini) to deliver the feature.

2.6 **Usage and analytics data.** Technical and usage information such as IP address, device/browser type, pages viewed, actions taken, timestamps, and diagnostic logs, used to operate, secure, and improve the Service.

2.7 **Communications.** Records of your communications with us (e.g., support requests and emails).
