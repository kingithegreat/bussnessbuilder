# Legal documents (DRAFTS)

These are **drafts for legal review — not legal advice.** They were prepared as a
starting point for BusinessFlow Studio and must be reviewed, completed, and
approved by qualified legal counsel before being published or relied upon.

| File | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| [`terms-of-service.md`](terms-of-service.md) | Terms of Service — accounts, billing/auto-renewal, refund policy, IP, **limitation of liability**, **indemnification**, termination, governing law |
| [`privacy-policy.md`](privacy-policy.md) | Privacy Policy — data collected, processors (Firebase/Stripe/Gemini/SMTP), controller/processor roles, retention, GDPR/CCPA, user rights |
| [`acceptable-use-policy.md`](acceptable-use-policy.md) | Acceptable Use Policy — prohibited content & activities, enforcement, reporting |

## Before publishing
Replace every bracketed placeholder, including:

- `[COMPANY LEGAL NAME]`
- `[JURISDICTION]` (governing law + venue)
- `[CONTACT EMAIL]` (and `[ABUSE EMAIL]`, `[DPO / PRIVACY CONTACT]` if used)
- `[EFFECTIVE DATE]`
- `[COMPANY ADDRESS]`
- `[SPECIFY PROVIDER]` (SMTP/email provider)

Counsel should also confirm: arbitration / class-action waiver (if desired), the
refund terms, the liability cap, international-transfer mechanisms, and CCPA
"sale/sharing" status.
